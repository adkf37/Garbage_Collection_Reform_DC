# Task 01 — Barcelona Shared Waste System Research

## Goal
Document how Barcelona’s shared container waste system works and what evidence
exists regarding efficiency, cleanliness, labor, and costs.

## Inputs
- Public reports, academic papers, city documentation

## Outputs
- research/barcelona_shared_waste.md

## Scope
- System design (containers, automation, staffing)
- Evidence on cost savings or labor reduction
- Evidence on cleanliness / recycling outcomes
- Known drawbacks or controversies
- Transferability notes for Washington, DC

## Constraints
- Distinguish evidence from claims
- Cite sources clearly

## Acceptance Criteria
- ≥3 credible sources cited
- Explicit list of assumptions
- Clear “what we know / don’t know” section
