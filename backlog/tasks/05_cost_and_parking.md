# Task 05 — Cost & Parking Impact Model

## Goal
Estimate startup and operating costs and curb/parking impacts.

## Outputs
- analysis/cost_model.py
- summary tables

## Scope
- Startup: trucks, containers, bin removal
- Operating: labor, maintenance, fuel
- Parking spaces or curb frontage consumed

## Constraints
- Separate capital vs operating costs
- Adjustable assumptions

## Acceptance Criteria
- Per-household and citywide figures
- Clear comparison to current system
